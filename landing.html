TIMELINE

Waveform Editor

Draw parameter curves directly on a beat-synced timeline. Every lighting dimension — dimmer, pan, tilt, color, gobo — lives on its own lane with visual cue blocks you can drag, resize, and ease.

FIXTURE

Multi-Fixture Control

Group fixtures by stage position. Apply effects across groups or individual heads. Full CMY, color wheel, and 16-bit pan/tilt support with real-time preview.

64 universes of Art-Net IV output with full ArtPollReply — LUMINA FX announces itself on your lighting network like any professional node. sACN support included.

Built-in stage visualizer shows fixture output in real-time. Color-mixed circles for every fixture, detailed moving head view for selected units — all in a separate window for zero performance impact.

Sine, chase, rainbow, strobe — built-in effect generators with per-fixture phase offset, speed control, and wave shaping. Stack effects or draw them manually.
